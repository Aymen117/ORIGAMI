Interesting Facts About Camels:

  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.
  • Camels can drink as much as 40 gallons of water at once.

Interesting Facts About Pigeons:

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  • Pigeons are highly sociable animals.

Interesting Facts About Chameleons:

  • Male and female chameleons have different coloring.
  • There are around 160 species of chameleon.
  • Half of all species of chameleon are found in Madagascar, Africa.

Interesting Facts About Cicadas:

  • Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs.
  • The loud whirring or buzzing sound you hear is an all-male cicada chorus.
  • Most cicadas have red-orange eyes.

Interesting Facts About Pandas:

  • Pandas are "lazy" — eating and sleeping make their day.
  • An adult can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day!
  • Scientists aren't sure how long pandas live in the wild, but in captivity they live to be around 30 years old.
